Welcome to Jot!

A deceptively simple note-taking application


Jot is a calendar based note-taking application ideal for making class notes, journaling, or jotting down any date sensitive ideas you may have.


Notes in Jot are powerred by html, but require no knowledge of html to edit. Jot does all the dirty work of coding your thoughts and ideas into html.


What does this mean for you?

This means you have all the power of html at your disposal with none of the hassel. Web links, file links, embedded pictures, ect can all be easily placed in your notes with a couple of mouse clicks.
